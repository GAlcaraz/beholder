NextDraft is a daily newsletter founded in 2010, with over 128,000 subscribers. It curates the most captivating and significant news stories of the day, providing a comprehensive and engaging read each morning.

![nextdraft](images/nextdraft.webp)

## Newsletter Features
NextDraft summarizes and contextualizes major news stories, offering unique insights that blend information with a touch of humor to keep subscribers entertained and informed.

## Writing Style
The writing style is engaging and playful, with a focus on making news accessible and enjoyable for the general reader. The newsletter strikes a balance between informative and entertaining content.

## Pricing
The newsletter is offered for free, making it accessible to a wide audience interested in staying updated with daily news highlights.

## Authors and Background
NextDraft is the brainchild of Dave Pell, a seasoned writer with a knack for boiling down complex stories into engaging summaries. He draws on his extensive experience in media and journalism to provide readers with a thoughtful selection of news stories.
