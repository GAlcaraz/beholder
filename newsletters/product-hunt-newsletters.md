Product Hunt offers newsletters perfect for those who want to stay on the cutting edge of the tech world. There are daily and weekly digests that cater to early adopters, entrepreneurs, creators, investors, and tech enthusiasts.

![product-hunt-newsletters](images/product-hunt-newsletters.webp)

## Newsletter Features
- **The Leaderboard**: A daily digest featuring trending products and tools.
- **The Roundup**: A weekly curated list showcasing innovations and startups, industry trends, and community feedback.

## Writing Style
Product Hunt newsletters are concise and curated, ensuring that readers receive impactful and relevant updates without overwhelming detail. Each edition is crafted to engage and inform tech-savvy audiences with the latest trends and tools.

## Pricing
Both newsletters from Product Hunt are provided for free, making them accessible to anyone interested in the latest in tech and startups.

## Authors and Background
The newsletters are created and curated by the team at Product Hunt, a well-known platform for discovering new products and startups. Their expertise provides readers with valuable insights into the industry.
