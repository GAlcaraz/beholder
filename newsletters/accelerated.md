Accelerated is a newsletter dedicated to students and recent graduates who are keen on venturing into the world of venture capital (VC). With over 36,000 subscribers since its inception in 2020, it provides valuable insights for young professionals looking to break into the VC industry.

![accelerated](images/accelerated.webp)

## Newsletter Features
Accelerated covers a broad range of topics related to venture capital, providing its audience with the latest trends, career advice, and entrepreneurial guidance tailored to early-stage professionals and students.

## Writing Style
The newsletter maintains an engaging and informative tone, making complex VC topics accessible to its target audience. It combines in-depth analyses with actionable career tips.

## Pricing
Accelerated is offered at no cost, making it an accessible resource for students and recent graduates.

The team behind Accelerated consists of industry experts and enthusiasts dedicated to demystifying venture capital for emerging talent.
